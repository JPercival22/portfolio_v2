Introduction

With over five years of experience, I have collaborated with start-ups and global corporations to design and develop intuitive web applications and mobile interfaces across various industries.

Most recently, as Senior UX Designer , Test Facilitator , and UI Designer at a data aggregation company, I led UX design initiatives and development projects used by the UK Government, the Metropolitan Police, and the National Crime Agency.

I hold a First-Class BSc (Hons) in Web Design and Development from Edge Hill University, which has shaped my approach to creating innovative digital solutions.

I am passionate about crafting meaningful user experiences and eager to contribute to projects that drive lasting impact.

Come Together Hub Ltd.

Bespoke Re-Design (UX&UI) & Development.

  • Lead UX Designer.
  • Lead UI Designer.
  • System Creator.
  • Web Master.

Come Together Hub, a St Helens-based charity in Merseyside (UK), required a functional and impactful online presence. After evaluating their original website, it became evident that a complete redesign and redevelopment was necessary. Aware of the charity’s limited resources and vital mission, I volunteered to lead the redesign and development and delivered a user-friendly, reliable, and visually engaging website that reflected their commitment to supporting the vulnerable.

Speech Recognition Research & Design (UX&UI).

  • UX & UI Designer.
