Hi, I'm Joseph Percival , Senior UX & UI Designer based in St. Helens, Merseyside (UK).

Introduction

With over four years of experience, I have had the privilege of collaborating with both innovative start-ups and established global corporations to design and develop exceptional user experiences. My expertise is centred on creating intuitive and groundbreaking web applications and mobile interfaces across a variety of platforms and industries.

Recently, I served as Senior UX Designer, Test Facilitator, and UI Designer at a data aggregation company. This multifaceted position involved leading UX design initiatives and overseeing development projects utilised by prominent organisations such as the UK Government, the Metropolitan Police, and the National Crime Agency. These opportunities have enabled me to deliver outstanding user experiences across multiple platforms.

My academic background at Edge Hill University, where I earned a First Class BSc (Hons) degree in Web Design and Development, has played a key role in shaping my career and driving my commitment to creating innovative digital solutions.

Looking ahead, I am eager to continue applying my skills and creativity to craft meaningful, impactful user experiences. I am passionate about contributing to projects that create significant digital experiences and make a lasting difference.

Case Studies

Come Together Hub Website Redesign and Development.

Responsive Re-design & Development Case Study.

  • Lead UX Designer.
  • Lead UI Designer.
  • Design System Creator.
  • Web Master.

Come Together Hub, a charity based in St Helens, Merseyside, required a functional and impactful online presence. After evaluating the issues with their existing website, it became clear that a complete redesign and redevelopment were essential. Recognising the charity’s limited resources and the significance of its mission, I volunteered to oversee both the redesign and development of the site. The goal was to create a user-friendly, dependable, and visually appealing website that truly represents the organisation’s dedication to supporting vulnerable individuals.

Just Speak Guitar Tutorials.

Research, Design & Development Case Study.

  • UX Researcher.
  • UX & UI Designer.
  • UX Test Facilitator.
  • Front End Developer.

Just Speak Guitar Tutorials is an innovative research and development project aimed at evaluating the integration of a non-AI voice command system within a responsive, content-rich website. The project applied UX design principles throughout the design and development phases, utilising user data gathered from interviews, surveys, and testing. By synthesising user needs and empathising with their experiences, the project effectively removed frustrations, addressed concerns, and maintained transparency at every stage.
