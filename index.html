Hi, I'm Joseph Percival, Senior UX & UI Designer based in St. Helens, Merseyside (UK).

Who I am and what I do.

I’m a multi-skilled, Senior, UX and UI Designer with a strong background in Front-End Development. I began my journey with earning a First-Class BSc (Hons) degree in Web Design and Development from Edge Hill University (UK). Gaining over five years of experience in the industry from graduating in 2019. Focusing on creating user-friendly digital experiences that improve engagement and drive business growth.

A major highlight of my career was working with The Hut Group Plc. There, I was responsible for UX design and development of their e-commerce platforms. I had the opportunity to work with brands like Calvin Klein, Adidas, and L’Oréal.

I’ve also supported Synalogik’s award-winning (The Princes Award for Innovation, 2023) platform, Scout. Scout is a data aggregation service that enhances intelligence and reconnaissance through automation. The platform is used by the UK government’s defence sector and global banks. I was the sole UX and UI designer for this project.

More recently, I worked with Come Together, a charity that supports the most vulnerable members of our society. As a pro-bono UX and UI designer, I helped the charity secure National Lottery funding, enabling them to expand their services significantly.

Viewing UX and UI design as analytical and creative disciplines, it is easy to see that it simplifies user journeys, enabling users to achieve their goals with minimal effort. From a business perspective, these principles are vital for strengthening customer relationships, creating marketing opportunities, and improving development processes.

My approach focuses on accessibility, data-driven user insights, and crafting compelling digital narratives. I thrive in team settings, where I can help shape and refine user experiences, increase engagement, and deliver impactful products. I take pride in developing innovative ideas that truly make a difference.

Come Together Hub Website Redesign and Development.

Responsive Re-design & Development Case Study.

  • Lead UX Designer.
  • Lead UI Designer.
  • Design System Creator.
  • Web Master.

Come Together Hub, a charitable organization in St Helens, Merseyside, needed a functional and impactful online presence. Following a thorough assessment of their existing website's issues, I determined that a complete redesign and redevelopment were necessary. Given their limited resources and importance of their mission, I offered to handle both the redesign and development of their website pro-bono. This project aimed to create a user-friendly, reliable, and visually appealing website that reflects the organization's dedication and commitment to supporting vulnerable individuals.

Just Speak Guitar Tutorials.

Research, Design & Development Case Study.

  • UX Researcher.
  • UX & UI Designer.
  • UX Test Facilitator.
  • Front End Developer.

Just Speak Guitar Tutorials is an innovative Research and Development Project focused on evaluating the integration of a non-AI voice command system into a responsive and content-rich website. The project seamlessly combined UX design methodologies during the design and development stages, leveraging user data gathered from interviews, surveys, and testing.
By synthesizing user requirements and empathizing with users, the project successfully eliminated frustrations, alleviated concerns, and ensured transparency at every phase of the process.
