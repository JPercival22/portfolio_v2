About Me

I blend experience and strategic design thinking to create visually compelling and functional experiences. From user research to polished prototypes, I ensure seamless, user-friendly products.


While earning my First-Class BSc in Web Design & Development , I was employed as a Student Mentor, honing skills in public speaking and teaching. As a father of two, I believe in helping others succeed.

I secured full-time employment before graduation, working with the NHS on health apps, then for a global corporation, creating marketing strategies for brands like Calvin Klein , Adidas , and Versace .

Recently, as Senior UX/UI Designer for a data aggregation business, I led the design of an e-commerce platform used by the UK Police , MI5 , and some major banks and insurers too. The company won the Prince’s Trust for Innovation during my tenure.


I also took on a Pro-Bono project and as such a lead role in the re-design and development of a local charity website in my spare time which help them achieve a greater online presence, modern communication tool and more importantly secured funding from the National Lottery and other investors.

I approach design with a mix of creativity and data-driven insights, always aiming to simplify user journeys and drive engagement. My focus is on creating meaningful, user-centric solutions that make a tangible difference. Looking ahead, I’m excited to continue using my skills to design innovative digital experiences that solve real-world challenges.
