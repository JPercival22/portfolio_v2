About Me

See Experience See Skills

Hi my names Joe and I’m a Senior UX & UI Designer with over five years’ experience in crafting intuitive, user-friendly digital experiences. With a strong background in Front-End Development , I combine technical expertise with creative problem-solving to design seamless, engaging interfaces. I hold a First-Class BSc (Hons) in Web Design and Development Degree from Edge Hill University (UK), where I developed the skills to bridge the gap between design and technology.

My career has given me the opportunity to work with leading brands like Calvin Klein, Adidas, and L’Oréal during my time at The Hut Group Plc . As part of the Graduate UX Design and Development team, I contributed to shaping high-performing E-commerce platforms, helping to create seamless shopping experiences for millions of users worldwide.

More recently, as the sole Senior UX & UI Designer at Synalogik Solutions Ltd , I played a key role in modernising and refining their user experience. Working closely with Architects, I helped improve the design of their award-winning data aggregation platform, SCOUT , which is used by the UK government and global financial institutions. I also redesigned and tested their E-Commerce platform to boost Data Set Sales and streamline client workflows.

One of the most rewarding projects I’ve worked on was with Come Together Hub , a local charity supporting vulnerable people in the community. As Lead Designer and Web Master , I redesigned and developed their website on a pro-bono basis. The site played a vital role in helping them secure National Lottery funding, allowing the charity to expand its services and support even more people in need.

I approach design with a mix of creativity and data-driven insights, always aiming to simplify user journeys and drive engagement. My focus is on creating meaningful, user-centric solutions that make a tangible difference. Looking ahead, I’m excited to continue using my skills to design innovative digital experiences that solve real-world challenges.
