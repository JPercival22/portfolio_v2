About Me

With over 4 years of experience, I've had the honor of collaborating with both innovative Start-Up Businesses and established Global Corporations to shape outstanding User Experiences. My specialization lies in the creation of intuitive and pioneering web applications and mobile interfaces across a diverse array of platforms and industries.

During my most recent tenure, I held a comprehensive role as a Senior UX Designer, Test Facilitator, and UI Designer at a Data Aggregation Company. This multifaceted position involved spearheading UX Design and overseeing Development Projects utilized by prestigious entities such as the UK Government, Metropolitan Police, and the National Crime Agency. These experiences empowered me to deliver exceptional User Experiences across an array of platforms.

My educational foundation at Edge Hill University, where I achieved a First Class BSc (Hons) Degree in Web Design and Development, has been pivotal in propelling my career toward the creation of innovative digital solutions.

Moving forward, I am enthusiastic about continuing to leverage my skills and creativity to craft impactful experiences for users. I am eager to contribute to meaningful endeavors and fulfill my passion for creating remarkable digital experiences.
